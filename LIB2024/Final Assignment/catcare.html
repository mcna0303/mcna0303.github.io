Cats can be finnicky creatures and it's important to ensure you're taking care of them as best you can! I've compiled a list of the most important Cat Care Rules!

5 Cat Care Tips

1. Choose a high quality wet and dry food!

Cats need food with a high percentage of protein because they are obligate carnivores! They also need a lot of moisture in their diet, as they're not good at drinking enough water! Giving them a high protein wet and dry food will set them up for success.

2. Keep their water source away from their food!

Cats still think and behave as if they were in the wild, so their water must be in a different place than their food! They also really like running water, so a filtered fountain is a good idea!

3. Brush your cats regularly!

Even though cats are really good at grooming themselves, it's always good to brush them at least once a week to help them with any dandruff or hard to reach spots!

4. Play time is a must!

If you're tired of your cat waking you up every night, then it's time to find out what kinds of toys they like! If you play with a cat, especially one over the age of 3, for about 30 minutes a day they'll be too tired to wake you up at the wee hours of the morning!

5. Cats need just as much love and attention as dogs do!

Lots of people think that cats are more independent or need less attention, but that's simply not true! It's not good to leave your cat alone for days at a time, so make sure you have a babysitter if you're going out of town! Cats thrive on routine and don't like it when their humans leave them for too long!
