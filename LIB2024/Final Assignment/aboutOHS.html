About the Ottawa Humane Society

The Ottawa Humane Society is the only officially operating animal shelter in the city! It services adopters from all over the Ottawa Valley and people from Hull and Gatineau in Quebec.

They do a lot of good work! I adopted Minerva from them via a Petsmart location. They work with local pet stores to get their adoptable cats seen!
